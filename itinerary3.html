Transportation 0/5

  • Book flights from international hub (e.g., PIT) → OSL (round trip) ~$800-1,200/person
  • Book flights TRD → US (return from Trondheim)
  • Book rental cars (2 cars, 10 days) - mid-size SUV or station wagon
  • Purchase AutoPASS for tolls and ferries
  • International Driving Permit (IDP) - AAA $30+ per driver

Lodging 0/6

  • Oslo hotel (Day 1) - Citybox $95 / Radisson RED $118 / Grand $285 per night
  • Lom hotel (Day 2) - Nordal Turistsenter $90 / Fossheim $140 / Røisheim $250 per night
  • Geiranger hotel (Days 3-4) - Grande Fjord $180 / Hotel Geiranger $240 / Hotel Union $320 per night
  • Ålesund hotel (Days 5-6) - Thon $236 / Quality $170 / Hotel Brosundet $220 per night
  • Molde hotel (Day 7) - Thon Moldefjord $130 / Molde Fjordhotell $150 / Scandic Seilet $200 per night
  • Trondheim hotel (Days 8-9) - City Living $110 / Clarion $150 / Radisson Blu $180 per night

  • Ringebu Stave Church (Day 2) - $10 (100 NOK)

Dining Reservations 0/7

  • Den Glade Gris or Fuglen (Oslo, Day 1) - popular Norwegian gastropub
  • Fossheim Hotel restaurant (Lom, Day 2) - traditional Gudbrandsdalen lamb stew
  • Brasserie Posten at Hotel Union (Geiranger, Day 3) - seafood platter
  • Apotekergata No. 5 (Ålesund, Day 6) - modern Norwegian, reserve
  • Bakklandet Skydsstation (Trondheim, Day 8) - cozy historic inn
  • Fagn (Trondheim, Day 9) - Michelin-starred local tasting menu, book well ahead
  • XL Diner (Ålesund, Day 5) - classic Norwegian seafood, bacalao
