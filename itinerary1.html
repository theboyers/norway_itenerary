Transportation 0/5

  • Book outbound flights PIT/MGW → OSL (1+ stops, 10-11+ hrs) ~$800-1,200/person
  • Book return flights OSL → US (11-13+ hours transit time)
  • Book rental cars (2 cars, 10 days) - mid-size SUV or station wagon
  • Register AutoPASS & FerryPay links vehicle tag for automated toll and fjord ferry billing
  • International Driving Permit (IDP) - Obtain via AAA $30+ per driver

Lodging 0/4

  • Oslo hotel center (Days 1, 9) - Citybox $95 / Radisson RED $118 / Grand $285 per night
  • Flåm base lodging (Days 2-4) - Flåm Marina $260 / Flåmsbrygga $235 / Fretheim $248 per night
  • Loen fjord lodging (Days 5-7) - Hotel Loenfjord $257 / Hotel Alexandra $380 per night
  • Lillehammer mountain camp (Day 8) - Sjusjøen Cabins $150 / Arctic Dome $333 / Scandic $220 per night

Activities 0/12

  • Oslo Waterfront Sauna (Day 1 or 9) - Oslo Badstuforening public slots - BOOKS 2 WEEKS OUT
  • National Museum Oslo (Day 1) - $22 (220 NOK) entry tickets
  • Borgund Stave Church (Day 2) - $15 (150 NOK) historical center admission
  • Flåm Railway round trip (Day 3) - $68 (730 NOK) - HIGH PRIORITY, book 2+ months ahead
  • Nærøyfjord UNESCO cruise (Day 3 or 4) - $64 (640 NOK) silent catamaran slots
  • Njord Kayak on Aurlandsfjord (Day 3) - $115 (1,235 NOK) guided tour
  • Aurlandsdalen Hikers' Bus (Day 4) - Reserve morning shuttle link from Flåm up to Østerbø
  • Loen Skylift boarding pass (Day 5-6) - 960 NOK (~$92) adult round trip
  • Briksdal Glacier troll car (Day 6) - 300 NOK (~$31) round trip transport slots
  • Via Ferrata Loen Climb (Day 6) - ~1,760 NOK ($165) - Strict safety caps, book far ahead
  • Lom Stave Church (Day 7) - $12 (120 NOK) traditional entry pass
  • Maihaugen, Fram, or Holmenkollen Museum (Days 8-9) - ~140-180 NOK entry options

  • Kest Tasting Room (Aurland, Day 3 or 4) - 8-course regional menu, weekend slots book early
  • Hotel Alexandra dinner buffet (Loen, Day 5 or 6) - Premier local seafood feast spread
  • LYNG mat & bar (Lillehammer, Day 8) - Highly-rated alpine seasonal kitchen, reserve
