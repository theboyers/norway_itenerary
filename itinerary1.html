Transportation 0/6

  • Book flights PIT/MGW → OSL (round trip) ~$800-1,200/person
  • Book return flights OSL → US (open-jaw or round trip)
  • Book rental cars (2 cars, 10 days) - mid-size SUV or station wagon
  • Purchase AutoPASS for tolls and ferries
  • Book Mannheller-Fodnes ferry (Day 5) ~$13 per car
  • International Driving Permit (IDP) - AAA $30+ per driver

Lodging 0/8

  • Oslo hotel (Days 1, 9) - Citybox $95 / Radisson RED $118 / Grand $285 per night
  • Flåm hotel (Days 2-4) - Flåm Marina $260 / Flåmsbrygga $235 / Fretheim $248 per night
  • Loen hotel (Days 5-7) - Hotel Loenfjord $257 / Hotel Alexandra $380 per night
  • Lillehammer area (Day 8) - Sjusjøen Cabins $150 / Arctic Dome $333 / Scandic $220 per night
