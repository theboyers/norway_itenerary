Transportation 0/6

  • Book flights PIT/MGW → OSL (round trip) ~$800-1,200/person
  • Book flights SVG → US (return from Stavanger)
  • Book rental cars (2 cars, 10 days) - mid-size SUV or station wagon
  • Purchase AutoPASS for tolls and ferries
  • Research ferry crossings (2x along route) - AutoPASS covers
  • International Driving Permit (IDP) - AAA $30+ per driver

Lodging 0/8

  • Oslo hotel (Day 1) - Citybox $95 / Radisson RED $118 / Grand $285 per night
  • Flåm hotel (Days 2-3) - Flåm Marina $260 / Flåmsbrygga $235 / Fretheim $248 per night
  • Bergen hotel (Days 4-5) - Citybox $110 / Zander K $150 / Hotel Norge $300 per night
  • Hardanger hotel (Days 6-7) - Eidfjord Gjestehus $120 / Ullensvang $220 / Hotel Ulvik $350 per night
  • Stavanger hotel (Days 8-9) - Stavanger B&B $100 / Thon Maritim $107 / Clarion $280 per night

Activities 0/12

  • Oslo Fjord sightseeing cruise (Day 1) - $30-50 (300-500 NOK)
  • Borgund Stave Church (Day 2) - $15 (150 NOK)
  • Flåm Railway round trip (Day 3) - $68 (730 NOK) - BOOKS OUT
  • Aurlandsfjord kayak (Day 3) - $90-110 guided
  • Voss Whitewater Rafting (Day 4) - $95-120
  • Fløibanen funicular (Day 5) - $20 (200 NOK) round trip
  • Mt. Ulriken cable car (Day 5) - $25 (250 NOK) round trip
  • Bondhusbreen Glacier hike (Day 6-7) - free, check conditions
  • Hardangerfjord cruise (Day 6-7) - $45-65
  • Hardanger Fruit Farm tour (Day 7) - $10-15 tasting
  • Preikestolen hike (Day 9) - free (parking $15), no guide needed
  • Lysefjord cruise (Day 9) - $45-65

Dining Reservations 0/6

  • Ægir Gastropub (Flåm) - Viking-themed, very popular, book ahead
  • Bryggen Tracteursted (Bergen) - historic 1710 Hanseatic wharf
  • Bare Vestland or Pingvinen (Bergen) - modern Norwegian or traditional pub
  • Ullensvang Hotel restaurant (Hardanger) - historic, reserve for fjord views
  • Hotel Ulvik Restaurant (Hardanger) - fine dining with fjord panorama
  • Fisketorget or Mølleren (Stavanger) - farewell dinner
