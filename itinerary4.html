Transportation 0/5

  • Book flights PIT/MGW → OSL (round trip) ~$800-1,200/person
  • Book flights BGO → US (return from Bergen)
  • Book rental cars (2 cars, 10 days) - mid-size SUV or station wagon
  • Purchase AutoPASS for tolls and ferries
  • International Driving Permit (IDP) - AAA $30+ per driver

Lodging 0/6

  • Oslo hotel (Days 1-2) - Citybox $95 / Radisson RED $118 / Grand $285 per night
  • Rjukan hotel (Days 3-4) - Rjukan Hytteby $110 / Rjukan Hotell $130 / Gausta View $170 per night
  • Hardanger hotel (Days 5-6) - Eidfjord Gjestehus $100 / Fjell & Fjord $160 / Quality Vøringfoss $220 per night
  • Bergen hotel (Days 7-9) - Citybox $137 / Zander K $150 / Hotel Norge $260 per night

Activities 0/10

  • National Museum Oslo (Day 1-2) - $20 (200 NOK)
  • Krokhol Disc Golf Tee Time (Day 2 Early Morning) - Book via UDisc app
  • Fram Museum (Day 2) - $16.82 (180 NOK)
  • Heddal Stave Church (Day 3) - $12 (120 NOK)
  • Vemork Heavy Water Museum (Day 4) - $14 (140 NOK)
  • Gaustabanen mountain tram (Day 4) - $56 (595 NOK) - limited capacity, book ahead
  • Rjukan Brewery tasting (Day 4) - $10-15 tasting flight
  • Bondhusbreen Glacier hike (Day 6) - free, check conditions
  • Voss Whitewater Rafting (Day 7) - $95-120 (950-1,200 NOK)
  • Fløibanen funicular + Ulriken cable car (Day 8) - $20 + $41
  • Fjord cruise to Mostraumen (Day 9) - $83 (890 NOK)

Dining Reservations 0/6

  • Mathallen Food Hall (Oslo, Day 1) - each person picks own vendor
  • Restaurant Schrøder (Oslo, Day 2) - historic 1910 Norwegian tavern
  • Gaustablikk Høyfjellshotell restaurant (Rjukan, Day 4) - mountain lodge dining
  • Bare Vestland or Pingvinen (Bergen, Day 7-9) - modern or traditional, reserve
  • Bryggen Tracteursted (Bergen, Day 9) - farewell dinner, 1708 Hanseatic building
  • Lysverket (Bergen, Day 9) - Michelin-listed Nordic, book ahead
