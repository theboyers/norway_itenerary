Transportation 0/7

  • Book flights PIT/MGW → OSL ~$800-1,200/person
  • Book flight BGO → LKN (Bergen to Leknes) - $80-150/person, Widerøe or SAS
  • Book flight LKN → US (return from Leknes via Oslo/Bodø)
  • Book Bergen Railway Oslo → Myrdal (Day 2) - ~$60/person
  • Book Flåm Railway Myrdal → Flåm (Day 2) - ~$70/person - SELLS OUT
  • Book rental car at Leknes (Day 6-10) - ~$400-800 per car, Lofoten cars go fast
  • International Driving Permit (IDP) - AAA $30+ per driver

Lodging 0/7

  • Oslo hotel (Day 1) - Citybox $95 / Radisson RED $118 / Grand $285 per night
  • Flåm hotel (Days 2-3) - Flåm Camping $90 / Flåm Marina $260 / Fretheim $248 per night
  • Bergen hotel (Days 4-5) - Citybox $137 / Zander K $231 / Hotel Norge $260 per night
  • Lofoten rorbu (Days 6-9) - Reine Rorbuer $181 / Eliassen $301 / Hamnøy $300 - BOOK 6+ MONTHS AHEAD

Dining Reservations 0/6

  • Ægir BrewPub (Flåm, Day 2) - legendary microbrewery, very popular, book ahead
  • Bryggen Tracteursted (Bergen, Day 4) - historic 1708 Hanseatic merchant house
  • Lysverket or Pingvinen (Bergen, Day 5) - Michelin-listed or budget traditional
  • 0Lofoten Food Studio (Lofoten, Day 8) - New Nordic tasting menu
  • Fiskekrogen (Reine, Day 9) - fine dining seafood
  • Mathallen Food Hall (Oslo, Day 1) - local craft stands
